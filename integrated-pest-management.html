Integrated Pest Management (IPM) is a smart and sustainable pest control strategy that combines inspection, monitoring, sanitation, and limited pesticide use. It’s best suited for industries, hospitals, schools, hotels, and large commercial spaces where long-term prevention and minimal chemical usage are priorities. IPM provides effective pest control while protecting health, property, and the environment.
